Who We Are

Local, hands-on,
and accountable.

Retro Data Recovery is a locally owned and operated service based in La Porte, Indiana. We started because we saw a real gap — most people have no idea what to do with old electronics, and even fewer know how much personal data is sitting on the drives inside them.

We come to you. We handle the heavy lifting. And we make sure every device is either safely recycled, every file is recovered, or every drive is destroyed beyond recovery — with documentation to prove it.

NIST 800-88 CERTIFIED

Our drive destruction meets the federal standard used by government agencies and healthcare providers. You get a signed Certificate of Destruction with every wipe or shred.

ZERO LANDFILL POLICY

Every device we collect goes to a certified municipal recycling facility. Nothing we pick up ends up in a dumpster or landfill — ever.

FAQ

Common questions.

Do you come to me?

Yes — we come to your home or business anywhere in La Porte County and surrounding areas. No need to haul anything yourself.

What happens to my data?

Drives are either wiped to NIST 800-88 standards or physically destroyed. You get a signed certificate confirming the method, serial number, and date.

How long does data recovery take?

Most logical recoveries are completed within 24–48 hours. We'll give you a go/no-go assessment before any charges for recovery work.

What electronics do you accept?

TVs, monitors, computers, laptops, phones, printers, routers, cables, hard drives, and most other consumer electronics. Not sure? Just ask.

Do you work with businesses?

Absolutely. We offer bulk pricing for businesses, medical offices, and legal firms disposing of IT assets. HIPAA-conscious handling available.

How do I get a quote?

Just call or text us with a rough description of what you've got. We'll get back to you with a flat-rate quote — usually same day.
